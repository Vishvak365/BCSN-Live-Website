What We Do

A little overview...

BCSN strives to provide the highest quality live streams of high school sports, all for the low price of free. Viewers can watch our past streams and highlight videos on youtube for no cost and can get notified when we are streaming on twitter. If you would like to support BCSN, please contact us at contact@bcsnlive.com

We have a goal, and that goal is very special...

Our underlying goal at BCSN is to bring awareness to our amazing athletes and get our school name out there more. Since we have stared, BCSN has had more than a dozen of our Barron Collier sports videos on our partner channel, NBC2.

NBC 2 supports BCSN

NBC 2 is a huge supporter of BCSN live! Since BCSN has started streaming, we have had more than a dozen videos on their news channel and there is more to come. These videos all highlight Barron Collier and the athletes of BC. Ever since we have partnered with NBC 2, Barron Collier is the most showed high school in all of Southwest Florida!
